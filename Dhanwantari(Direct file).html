About Dhanwantari

Dhanwantari is a **multilingual AI chatbot** designed to empower rural and semi-urban communities with vital health information. Our mission is to bridge the healthcare knowledge gap by providing accessible, reliable, and easy-to-understand guidance on preventive care, disease symptoms, and essential vaccination schedules.

The chatbot seamlessly integrates with **government health databases**, enabling it to deliver real-time, localized alerts about disease outbreaks and public health advisories. We believe that by providing this critical information directly to people via platforms they already use, we can significantly improve community health outcomes and foster a more proactive approach to wellness.

Dengue Fever Awareness

Dengue fever is a viral infection transmitted by mosquitoes. Symptoms often include a high fever, severe headache, and joint pain. To prevent it, use repellents and eliminate stagnant water.

Respiratory Virus Season

Respiratory viruses spread easily through the air. Protect yourself by practicing good hygiene, such as frequent handwashing and avoiding touching your face. Getting a flu vaccine is highly recommended.
